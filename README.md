## Zaathi – Your Digital Health Companion

Zaathi is a health-tech platform designed to bridge the information gap between doctors, patients, and family caregivers. It ensures that medical instructions, medication schedules, and health updates remain accessible, accurate, and easy to follow beyond hospital visits.

By combining smart reminders, voice assistance, and real-time monitoring, Zaathi promotes safer, more coordinated, and continuous healthcare.

---

## Career Objective

To design and develop user-friendly healthcare solutions that improve patient safety and caregiver coordination through technology. This project reflects my goal of applying software engineering skills to create reliable, impactful, and socially responsible digital systems while continuously learning and growing as a developer.

---

## Features

* Medication reminders to prevent missed or duplicate doses
* Voice assistant for easy interaction
* Real-time monitoring for caregivers
* Digital doctor notes and summary reports for follow-up visits
* Medicine stock tracking to prevent shortages
* Multilingual support for diverse users
* Emergency alert system for critical situations

---

## Purpose

Zaathi aims to:

* Reduce caregiver stress
* Prevent medical errors
* Improve patient adherence
* Enable data-driven medical consultations
* Ensure continuity of care outside hospitals
